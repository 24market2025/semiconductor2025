IC CMP Slurries Key Market Trends  :
Growing Demand for Advanced Nodes

With the rise of AI, IoT, and 5G, there is an increasing demand for smaller and more powerful semiconductor nodes, boosting the need for high-quality CMP slurries.
Rise in 3D NAND and DRAM Production

The expansion of memory technologies like 3D NAND and DRAM is driving demand for CMP slurries optimized for these advanced architectures.
Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Formulations

Market players are focusing on developing environmentally friendly CMP slurries with reduced chemical waste and lower energy consumption.
Shift Toward Advanced Materials

The industry is seeing a transition from traditional materials to new compositions like ceria-based slurries for improved polishing efficiency.
Expansion of Semiconductor Foundries

Increased investments in semiconductor fabrication facilities, especially in Asia-Pacific and North America, are creating a higher demand for IC CMP slurries.
