Hall Switch Market Overview
Hall switches are active element magnetoelectric transducers. It is based on the Hall effect principle and is used in the production process of integrated packaging and assembly. It converts convenient magnetic signals into electrical signals. The Hall effect refers to the phenomenon that when a solid conductor is placed in a magnetic field and a current passes through it, the charge carriers in the conductor are biased to one side by the Lorentz force, and then a voltage (Hall voltage) is generated. The electric force induced by the voltage balances the Lorentz force. Through the polarity of the Hall voltage, it can be confirmed that the current inside the conductor is caused by the movement of negatively charged particles (free electrons).
