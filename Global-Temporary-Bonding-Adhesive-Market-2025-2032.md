Temporary Bonding Adhesive Market Overview

Temporary bonding adhesives are specialized adhesives designed to provide a temporary bond between two surfaces, allowing them to be separated later without leaving residue or causing damage. These adhesives are often used in the electronics and semiconductor industries, particularly in processes such as wafer thinning, handling, and packaging. They provide reliable adhesion during critical manufacturing steps and are removed using heat, UV light, or chemical solutions.
