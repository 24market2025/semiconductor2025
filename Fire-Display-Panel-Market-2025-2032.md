Fire Display Panel Market Overview
Fire Display Panel is a digital fire alarm display device designed and developed with a single-chip microcomputer that can be installed on floors or independent fire zones. It has multiple functions such as fire alarm, fault display, and supervision alarm display, which is of great significance for improving the reliability and safety of fire protection systems.
