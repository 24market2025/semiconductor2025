SWIR Hyperspectral Remote Sensing Key Market Trends :

Growth in Defense and Security Applications: SWIR hyperspectral remote sensing is gaining traction in defense and security sectors for advanced surveillance and threat detection.

Emergence of Compact and Portable SWIR Sensors: The development of smaller, more portable sensors is expanding the use of SWIR technology in field applications.
