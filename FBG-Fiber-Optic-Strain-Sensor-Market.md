FBG Fiber Optic Strain Sensor Market Overview
FBG Fiber Optic Strain Sensor is a high-precision, high-sensitivity strain measurement device based on fiber Bragg grating (FBG) technology, usually composed of optical fiber, Bragg grating and packaging materials. FBG fiber optic strain sensor has the characteristics of high precision, high sensitivity and strong anti-interference ability, and is widely used in construction, energy, aerospace and other fields.
