FRED Modules Market Overview
FRED (Framework for Reconstructing Epidemiological Dynamics) modules are specialized components designed for agent-based modeling (ABM) of infectious diseases and population dynamics. These modules facilitate data-driven simulations, allowing researchers to study the spread of diseases, intervention strategies, and healthcare impacts in diverse populations.
