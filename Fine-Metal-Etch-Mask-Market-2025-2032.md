Fine Metal Etch Mask Market Overview
Fine metal etch masks are key materials in the manufacture of semiconductor chips. Fine metal etching masks are used in lithography steps in wafer manufacturing or other circuit printing. In the process of semiconductor manufacturing, multiple photolithography processes are usually required to open various doping windows on the dielectric layer on the surface of semiconductor crystals. , electrode contact holes, or etching metal interconnection patterns on the conductive layer, a complete set of photocopying masks with specific patterns that can be accurately registered with each other is required, and its precision is directly related to the quality of the circuit and the final product.
