EMI Suppression Film Capacitors for Power Supply Market Overview
EMI suppression polypropylene film capacitors are a type of film capacitor that are specifically designed to suppress electromagnetic interference (EMI) in electronic circuits. They are made of a thin layer of metalized polypropylene film, which serves as the dielectric material.
