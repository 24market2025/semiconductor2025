Paper and Plastic Film Capacitors Market Overview
Film capacitors, also known as film dielectric capacitors, plastic film capacitors, polymer film capacitors, film caps or power film capacitors, are electrical capacitors that contain an insulating plastic film which is the dielectric, and is sometimes combined with paper as the carrier of electrodes.
