Glass Optical Lenses Market Overview
Glass optical lenses are composed of multiple glass elements and are renowned for their high light transmittance. Due to the stringent requirements in molding techniques, coating processes, and precision machining, these lenses are predominantly used in high-end imaging applications. Such applications include DSLR cameras, high-end scanners, automotive imaging systems, and advanced security surveillance equipment.
