External AC-DC Power Supplies Adapters Market Overview
External AC-DC power supply adapters, often referred to simply as power adapters or power supplies, are devices that convert alternating current (AC) electrical energy from a wall outlet into direct current (DC) energy that can be used to power various electronic devices. These adapters are commonly used to provide the correct voltage and current required by electronic devices that operate on DC power, such as laptops, smartphones, routers, gaming consoles, and other consumer electronics.
